Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

Introduction

At Utilities 4 Dragon, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services, including Dictation Box and Speech Commander.

Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and payment details (processed via PayPal).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our software, such as commands used and session duration.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and operating system.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our Services.
  • Process payments and manage your account.
  • Send you updates, newsletters, and support information (you can opt out at any time).

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • PayPal, to process payments securely.
  • Service providers who assist with website hosting and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website, such as remembering your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@utilities4dragon.com.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at support@utilities4dragon.com or call +1 (234) 567-890.
